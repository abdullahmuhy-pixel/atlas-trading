QUESTIONS

Frequently asked

How many signals are sent per week? +
Typically 3–8 signals per week, depending on market conditions. Quality is the priority — a week with no valid setups is better than forcing low-probability trades. Signals cover the London open and NY open windows, which are the highest-probability times for South African traders.
Do I need trading experience? +
Basic familiarity with MetaTrader 5 and how to place orders is helpful. Each signal includes the order type (market, buy limit, sell stop etc.) and all price levels, so you don't need to analyse charts yourself. We recommend starting with the free trial to learn the format before going live with real money.
Which broker do I need? +
Signals are optimised for XM broker on MT5 (the most widely used broker for our instruments across SA, Africa, the Middle East, and Europe). The lot sizing guidance is calibrated for XM's nano account. Any MT5 broker offering gold, indices, and forex will work — just adjust position sizes for your broker's contract specifications. XM accepts clients from over 190 countries.
Are results guaranteed? +
No. Trading involves risk and past performance does not guarantee future results. Every signal has a defined stop loss — losing trades are a normal part of any trading strategy. The edge comes from consistently positive expectancy over many trades, not from winning every single one. Our track record is fully transparent so you can judge the historical performance yourself.
How do I receive the signals? +
After subscribing, you receive an invite to a private Telegram channel within minutes. Signals, TP/SL update messages, and weekly briefs (Pro plan) all arrive in that channel. Enable Telegram notifications so you don't miss entries.
How do I cancel? +
Message us directly on WhatsApp or Telegram and cancellation happens immediately. No notice period, no penalty. You keep access until the end of the billing period you've paid for.
What minimum account size do I need? +
Signals include lot size guidance for accounts from R1,000 (XM nano) upward. A larger account gives you more sizing flexibility and can absorb drawdown periods more comfortably — R5,000+ is ideal to follow all signals at 1% risk per trade.
