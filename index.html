WHAT IS ATLAS?
ATLAS is your personal institutional market intelligence tool. It thinks like a senior trader at Goldman Sachs or JPMorgan -- analysing live market data, computing technical indicators, reading macro fundamentals, and giving you a structured trade plan. You do the trading on XM Trader. ATLAS does the analysis.
STEP 1 -- SETUP (ONE TIME)
1.Go to console.anthropic.com -- sign up free -- click API Keys -- Create Key -- copy it
2.Paste your key into the CONNECT AI ENGINE banner at the top -- tap SAVE
3.The banner disappears. ATLAS is now connected. Cost is ~$0.01-0.03 per full analysis.
Your key is stored only in your phone browser. It is never sent anywhere except directly to Anthropic when you run an analysis.
STEP 2 -- DAILY WORKFLOW
AMBefore the session opens: Go to DAILY PLAN tab -- enter your instrument, account size, risk % -- tap GENERATE. This gives you your complete game plan for the day: go/no-go decision, entry, stop, targets, and news to avoid.
PREBefore each trade: Type your instrument in the search bar -- tap ANALYSE. Read all 4 tabs: Overview, Technical, Fundamental, Tactics.
CHARTConfirm visually: Open the CHART tab and verify the levels ATLAS identified are visible on the chart. Draw your entry, SL, and TP on the chart.
TRADEExecute on XM Trader: Open your XM MT4/MT5 app -- enter the trade exactly as ATLAS specified -- set SL and TP.
LOGAfter closing: Go to TRADES tab -- log the trade with your P&L, strategy used, and notes. ATLAS tracks your performance over time.
OVERVIEW TAB -- WHAT IT SHOWS
BiasBullish / Bearish / Neutral with confidence score. This is ATLAS overall verdict on the instrument right now.
3 TimeframesIntraday (today), Swing (this week), Macro (this month). All 3 bullish = strong signal. Conflicting = be careful.
Key ThesisThe single most important thing driving price right now -- the main reason to buy or sell.
Smart Money ViewWhat institutions and big banks are likely positioned for. Follow this, not retail sentiment.
Key LevelsThe most important support and resistance prices. These are where you set your entries, stops, and targets.
Primary RiskThe main thing that could make this trade wrong. If this happens, exit immediately.
Watch ForThe price event that would change everything -- e.g. a break above key resistance changes bearish to bullish.
TECHNICAL TAB -- WHAT IT SHOWS
Trend StructureUptrend, downtrend, or ranging. Based on live price structure from 5-day hourly data.
RSI(14)Above 70 = overbought (price may fall). Below 30 = oversold (price may rise). Computed from live data.
MACDShows momentum direction. Bullish cross = upward momentum. Bearish cross = downward momentum.
EMA AlignmentEMA20 above EMA50 above EMA200 = strong uptrend. Reverse = strong downtrend. Mixed = ranging market.
Support/ResistanceKey price levels where price has previously reversed. These are your trade levels.
Most WatchedThe single technical factor that 80% of traders are watching on this instrument right now.
FUNDAMENTAL TAB -- WHAT IT SHOWS
Macro RegimeThe big picture economic environment. Risk-on vs risk-off. Which central banks are hiking or cutting rates.
Key DriversThe 3-5 fundamental factors moving this instrument most right now -- ranked by impact.
Central BankFor forex: the interest rate differential between the two currencies. Higher rate currency tends to strengthen.
Data to WatchUpcoming economic releases that will move this market. Avoid trading 30 min before and after these.
CorrelationsRelated markets that confirm or contradict the trade. E.g. Gold rises when DXY falls.
TACTICS TAB -- WHAT IT SHOWS
DirectionLONG (buy) or SHORT (sell). This is what ATLAS recommends for this instrument right now.
EntryThe specific price or zone to enter the trade. Wait for price to reach here -- do not chase.
Entry TriggerThe exact condition that must happen before you enter -- e.g. a candle close above resistance.
Stop LossWhere to put your stop. If price hits this, the trade is wrong. Always set this on XM before anything else.
TP1 / TP2First and second profit targets. Close 50-70% of the position at TP1, let the rest run to TP2.
R:R RatioReward vs Risk. 1:2 means you risk $1 to make $2. Never take a trade below 1:1.5.
InvalidationThe price that proves the analysis wrong. If hit, exit immediately -- no questions asked.
Best SessionLondon and New York overlap (13:00-17:00 UTC) is peak liquidity for most instruments.
DAILY PLAN TAB -- HOW TO USE
Run this every morning before you open XM Trader. It generates your complete day in one shot.
InstrumentWhat you plan to trade today -- e.g. XAUUSD
Account ($)Your actual XM account balance right now
Risk/Trade %How much % of account to risk per trade. 1% is standard. Never go above 2%.
YesterdayIf you had losses yesterday, ATLAS will reduce risk today. This prevents revenge trading.
GO = trade today normally. CAUTION = reduce size. NO TRADE = stay out entirely. Respect this decision -- it is based on your account state and market conditions.
TRADES TAB -- HOW TO USE
Log every trade after you close it on XM. This is how you track whether you are actually making money and improving.
P&L ($)Positive = profit. Negative = loss. Enter the exact dollar amount from XM.
Win Rate% of trades that were profitable. 50%+ with good R:R = profitable system.
Profit FactorTotal wins divided by total losses. Above 1.5 = strong. Below 1 = losing system.
Drawdown GuardEnter your account size and max daily loss. ATLAS tells you when to stop trading for the day automatically.
TOOLS TAB -- WHAT IS THERE
Pip CalculatorEnter pair + lot size + stop loss in pips. Shows exact dollar risk. Use before every trade.
Position SizerEnter account, risk %, entry and stop price. Shows exact number of units/lots to trade.
Market PulseLive prices for DXY, VIX, Gold, Oil, 10Y yield, Bitcoin. Check these before any trade.
Central Bank RatesCurrent interest rates for all major currencies. Higher rate = stronger currency (generally).
EA GeneratorGenerates MQL4/MQL5 code you paste into MT4/MT5 MetaEditor. Creates an alert bot or auto-trader for your strategy.
GOLDEN RULES
1Never trade without a stop loss set on XM. Ever.
2Never risk more than 1-2% of your account on one trade.
3If you hit your daily loss limit in ATLAS, stop trading immediately.
4Run DAILY PLAN every morning before opening XM. Do not skip this.
5ATLAS analysis is valid for 4-24 hours. Re-run before each new session.
6If ATLAS says NO TRADE -- do not trade. Walk away.
7Log every trade. The performance data is your most valuable feedback.
